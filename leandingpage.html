How It Works

From Lead to Deal in 4 Steps

01

Lead Comes In

A prospect fills out a form, clicks an ad, or calls in. LeadPilot captures them instantly from any source — Zillow, your website, Facebook, Google, you name it.

02

AI Qualifies & Scores in Real Time

The system analyzes intent, budget signals, and engagement patterns. Every lead gets a score — hot, warm, or cold — before your agent even sees it.

03

Automated Outreach Kicks In

AI calls the lead, sends personalized texts and emails, and nurtures them on autopilot. Your team only steps in when the lead is ready.

04

Showing Booked → Deal Closed

AI schedules the property showing. Your agent walks in prepared. You close. Simple, repeatable, scalable.
